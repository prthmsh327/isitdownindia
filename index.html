🛠 Troubleshooting — Can't Access This Site?

1. Force Refresh Your Browser

Press Ctrl + Shift + R (Windows) or Cmd + Shift + R (Mac) to bypass your browser's cache and reload fresh.

2. Flush Your DNS Cache

On Windows: Press Win + R → type cmd → run ipconfig /flushdns. This clears stale DNS entries that your Jio/Airtel/BSNL router may have cached.

3. Switch DNS to Google or Cloudflare

Your ISP's DNS may be slow or blocking certain sites. Try Google DNS: 8.8.8.8 / 8.8.4.4 or Cloudflare DNS: 1.1.1.1. Set this in your WiFi settings.

4. Try Mobile Data vs WiFi

Switch between Jio WiFi and mobile data (or vice versa). If it works on one and not the other, the issue is with that specific ISP or network, not the site.

5. Clear Browser Cookies & Cache

In Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data. Select "Cookies" and "Cached images" then clear. Works for most stuck-login or broken-page issues.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why does Swiggy's website show UP but the app isn't working? +
Apps and websites run on different infrastructure. Swiggy's homepage may be fine while their payment or delivery API is failing — our Report button catches this.
Why is a site working for others but not for me in India? +
Some sites are blocked by Indian ISPs (Jio, Airtel, BSNL) or by government order. Sometimes only specific regions or networks are affected — which is why testing from your own browser matters.
How is this different from global down checkers? +
Global tools ping from US/Europe servers. We check from India and combine that with real user reports from Indian users — giving you results relevant to your actual experience.
What does "Last Down" mean? +
"Last Down" shows the last time our tool detected this site as unreachable from India, based on checks stored on your device. This helps identify recurring outage patterns.
