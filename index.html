❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why does a website show UP but the app isn't working? +
Apps and websites run on different infrastructure. A service's homepage may be fine while their payment servers, login APIs, or delivery systems are failing. Our crowdsource report button is designed to catch exactly this kind of issue.
Why is a site working for others but not for me in India? +
Some sites are blocked by Indian ISPs (Jio, Airtel, BSNL, Vi) or by government order. Sometimes only specific regions or networks are affected — testing from your own browser matters more than a server-side check.
How is this different from global down checkers? +
Global tools ping from servers in the US or Europe. We check directly from your browser in India, and combine that with real user reports from Indian users — giving you results that reflect your actual network experience.
What does the 24-hour outage chart show? +
The chart shows the last 24 checks for that site — green bars mean UP, red bars mean DOWN, yellow means slow. Hover a bar to see the exact response time and timestamp. This helps you identify recurring outage patterns.
Is this tool free? +
Yes — completely free. No login, no account, no personal information needed.

🛠 Can't Access This Site? Try These Fixes

1. Force Refresh

Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) to reload without browser cache.

2. Flush DNS Cache

Windows: Press Win+R → type cmd → run ipconfig /flushdns. Clears stale Jio/Airtel/BSNL DNS entries.

3. Switch to Google or Cloudflare DNS

In your WiFi settings, set DNS to 8.8.8.8 (Google) or 1.1.1.1 (Cloudflare). Your ISP's DNS may be blocking or slow.

4. Switch Between WiFi and Mobile Data

If it works on Airtel data but not Jio WiFi (or vice versa), the issue is ISP-specific — not the site itself.

5. Clear Browser Cache & Cookies

Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → select Cookies + Cached images.
