❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why does Swiggy's website show UP but the app isn't working? +
Apps and websites run on different infrastructure. Swiggy's homepage may be fine while their payment servers or delivery API is failing — our Report button catches exactly this.
Why is a site working for others but not for me in India? +
Some sites are blocked by Indian ISPs (Jio, Airtel, BSNL) or by government order. Sometimes only specific regions are affected, which is why testing from your own browser matters.
How is this different from global down checkers? +
Global tools ping from servers in the US or Europe. We check from India and combine that with real user reports from Indian users — giving you results relevant to your actual experience.
How many reports trigger an alert? +
30 or more user reports in 10 minutes triggers a "Reported Issues" override — even if the technical ping says UP.
Is this tool free? +
Yes — completely free. No login, no account, no tracking needed.
