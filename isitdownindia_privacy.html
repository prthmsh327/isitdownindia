Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Short version: We don't ask for your name, email, or any personal account. We use Google AdSense for ads and collect standard anonymous usage data. We don't sell your data.

1. Who We Are

IsItDownIndia.in ("we", "us", "our") is a free website status checker designed for Indian internet users. We help you check whether a website is down, blocked, or slow in India.

For questions about this policy, contact us at: hello@isitdownindia.in

2. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use IsItDownIndia.

What we do collect:

  • Website check requests: When you check a URL, that URL is processed to return a status result. We may log anonymized check requests for performance monitoring and abuse prevention.
  • Problem reports: When you click "I'm Having a Problem," we record the site domain and timestamp. We do not associate this with your identity.
  • Browser local storage: We store a small flag in your browser's local storage to remember that you've already voted for a site in the current session. This never leaves your device.
  • Standard server logs: Like all websites, our servers may log your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. These are used for security and analytics only and are not sold.

3. Google AdSense & Cookies

IsItDownIndia uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, visit: google.com/policies/privacy/partners

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate this site:

  • Google AdSense — for advertising. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare — for server infrastructure and security. May process request data per Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — for storing anonymized crowdsource vote counts (site domain + timestamp only). Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy.

We do not share personally identifiable information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Cookies

We use cookies in the following ways:

  • Functional cookies: Browser local storage flags to remember your vote per site (stored on your device only).
  • Advertising cookies: Set by Google AdSense to serve relevant ads.
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous usage statistics to understand how visitors use the site.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the site.

6. Children's Privacy

IsItDownIndia is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information we collect. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you not to share sensitive personal information through any website.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data tied to your identity, there is generally no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have concerns about any data we may hold, contact us at hello@isitdownindia.in and we will respond within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of IsItDownIndia after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
